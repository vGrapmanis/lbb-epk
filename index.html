Latvian Blues Band – European Blues Powerhouse

The Latvian Blues Band (LBB) is one of Europe’s most recognized blues acts — a dynamic, high-energy group with more than two decades of experience performing on world stages.

Founded in Riga in 2000, the band’s journey began with a collaboration with Canadian blues legend Johnny V, followed by an extensive concert tour across Canada — from Toronto to Calgary. Since then, the LBB has become a respected name on the international blues scene.

Highlights & Achievements

  • Performed in over 20 countries including the USA, Canada, Germany, Spain, Greece, Denmark, and Egypt.
  • Featured in Blues Revue Magazine as a “European blues phenomenon” by Bob Margolin, legendary guitarist for Muddy Waters.
  • Winners of the Baltic Blues Challenge (2009), earning a place in the International Blues Challenge finals (2010, Memphis, Tennessee).
  • Invited to perform at the Chicago Blues Festival – the first European-based band in the festival’s history.
  • Collaborations with world-renowned artists such as Duke Robillard, Raphael Wressnig, Bob Margolin, Justina Lee Brown and others.
  • Multiple acclaimed album releases, including:
    • Unreal (2009, produced by Duke Robillard)
    • Latvian Blues Band & Friends – Live at the Dream Factory (2013) – Golden Microphone Award winner for Best Jazz/Blues Album
  • Featured in festivals and venues across Europe and North America, captivating audiences with authentic blues energy and stage charisma.

Celebrating its 25th anniversary, the Latvian Blues Band continues to deliver unforgettable live performances across Scandinavia and Europe, blending traditional blues roots with modern groove and soulful precision.

The LBB’s shows are a guaranteed highlight for any festival line-up — energetic, professional, and deeply rooted in the spirit of the blues.
